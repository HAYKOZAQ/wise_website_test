23 Years of Experience

We Dream, We Create, We Share

The Welfare Information Systems Enterprise Foundation is a leading technology center in Armenia, implementing and maintaining information and telecommunication technology infrastructures for both the public and private sectors.

Approximately 1,109,493 active beneficiaries receive services through the information systems we provide in the field of social protection of the Republic of Armenia.
